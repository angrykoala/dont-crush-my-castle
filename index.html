Welcome to Don´t Crush my Castle

Don't Crush my Castle (DCmC) is an open-source and highly-customizable tower defense (currently at alpha 0.7 version with a lot of juicy stuff that will be included in the future by some of DCmC contributors to make it a next-gen of tower-defense games


DCmC alpha 0.6 screenshot

Don't Crush My Castle is being developed in C++ and Allegro 5 and Tiled

Is DCmC currently working?
